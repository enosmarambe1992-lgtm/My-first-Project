Our Story

Rooted in the Soil of Kakamega

E&M Fresh Harvests was born from a simple belief — that every family deserves access to fresh, clean, and nutritious food grown with love and respect for the land.

Nestled along Mumias Road in Kakamega, our farm has been nurturing the soil and our community for years. We grow a wide range of produce — from everyday vegetables and grains to premium nuts, oilseeds, and specialty crops.

Every product you see here is grown by us, harvested at peak freshness, and brought straight to your table — no long supply chains, no compromises.

✅ No pesticides or harmful chemicals
✅ Harvested within 24 hours of your order
✅ Supporting local farmers and families
✅ Fair prices direct from the farm
